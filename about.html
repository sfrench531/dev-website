About Muxy

Muxy began as a company developing high-performing Twitch streamer tools. While we do still own and maintain our streamer tools, our focus as a company has shifted to interactive live video applications (ILV apps for short). Our current project is developing a comprehensive backend solution for extension development, the Muxy SDK. Muxy also undertakes some in-house extension development contracts, using our SDK as the backbone of all custom work.
